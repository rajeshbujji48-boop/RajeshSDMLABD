FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with us.

Most clients receive their first qualified leads within 7–14 days of campaign launch. Google Search Ads deliver the fastest results since they target people actively searching for your service right now. Meta and LinkedIn Ads typically take 2–3 weeks to optimise as the algorithm learns your best audience. Full campaign maturity is reached around day 30–60.
We recommend a minimum monthly ad spend of ₹30,000 for Google Ads, ₹25,000 for Meta Ads, and ₹50,000 for LinkedIn Ads. Below these amounts there isn't enough data for the algorithms to optimise effectively. Our management fee is separate from your ad spend — you pay the platforms directly, so every rupee goes to your ads.
You always own 100% of your ad accounts. Your Google Ads, Meta Business Manager, and LinkedIn Campaign Manager accounts stay in your name. We only request manager-level access to run campaigns on your behalf. If you ever decide to leave, you keep everything — your accounts, your data, your history. No lock-ins, ever.
We set up Google Tag Manager and conversion tracking on day one — tracking phone calls, form submissions, WhatsApp clicks, and purchases. You get a live dashboard showing cost per lead, lead quality, and ROAS in real time. Every month you receive a detailed report with actual revenue metrics — not just impressions and clicks.
We agree on clear KPIs before we start — target cost per lead, lead volume, or ROAS — so both sides know exactly what "working" means. You get full visibility into performance from day one through your live dashboard, and we review progress together every month. If a campaign isn't hitting targets, we adjust strategy quickly rather than letting budget sit on something that isn't working — we only take on clients we're confident we can grow.
